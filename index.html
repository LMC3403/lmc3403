Poetry Vending Machine

Let's start scrolling and learn more about how to use the Poetry Vending Machine.

Overview

The Poem Vending machine is used to distribute poems.The machine allows organizations to distribute poems of their choosing and the categories they wish to group the poems in. Poetry is one of those topics that often gets dismissed as whimsical and unimportant especially among the youth age group. However, it is a powerful instructional tool and an important art to study at any age. It is a way to understand how language and symbol systems work. It is a worthy expression of emotion, or deep feelings, and aesthetics, or a sense of what is beautiful about the world. Our machine aims:

  1. Give everyone an opportunity to explore different genres and poets.
  2. Share their thought of the poem with other users.
  3. Share their own poem with others.
The machine should be placed in an open areas, where people are constantly walking by and will be able to quickly get a poem dispensed to them. It provides an avenue to helping youth and adults become interested in poetry.

Description of Equipment

       




Using coils, we will make a poetry dispenser modeled after a vending machine. Paper strips with poems on them will be rolled up and placed in coils. There's three handles, one for each coil, that the user can turn to rotate the coil and pushes a poem to the front, causing it to fall and be released. This style machine also allows the user to reload poems and separate them into categories.

Materials and Equipment




















Lids

Number Needed: 3










Cardboard Box

Number Needed: 4









Metal Spiral Coils

Number Needed: 3








Hot Glue Gun and Glue

1 Glue Gun
Number of Glue Sticks Needed: 5

Directions






Step 1

Get a piece or strip of paper with a poem on it and roll the paper.

Step 2

Put it into a piece of a straw. (You can do this with several pieces of paper)

Step 3

Open the top of the box and place the straws in the gaps of the coil.

Step 4

Rotate the knob to turn the coil. As the coil turns eventually a straw will fall from the coil and into the tray below

For Refilling

Repeat step 1~3 with new poems or the same ones that fell out.
