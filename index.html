Overview

The Poem Vending Machine is used to distribute poems. The machine allows organizations to distribute poems of their choosing and the categories they wish to group the poems in. The aim is to help distribute poems to more youth to give them an opportunity to explore different genres and poets. The machine should be placed in an open areas, where people are constantly walking by and will be able to quickly get a poem dispensed to them. Poetry is not widely appreciated by younger adults and age groups. Therefore, the machine should be used to help motivate these age groups to begin reading and gaining interests in poetry. Poetry is one of those topics that often gets dismissed as whimsical and unimportant. However, it is a powerful instructional tool and an important art to study at any age. Poetry is a way to understand how language and symbol systems work. It is a worthy expression of emotion, or deep feelings, and aesthetics, or a sense of what is beautiful about the world. Therefore, this machine provides an avenue to helping youth and adults become interested in poetry.
